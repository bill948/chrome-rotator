Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

In short: Kiosk Tab Rotator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

About This Extension

Kiosk Tab Rotator is a Chrome browser extension that automatically cycles through your open tabs after a period of user inactivity. It is designed for dashboards, kiosks, digital signage, and similar unattended display use cases.

Data Collection

This extension collects no personal data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No browsing history is collected or transmitted
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is accessed
  • No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking is performed
  • No cookies are read or written by the extension
  • No data is sent to any external server or third party

Data Storage

The extension stores only three user-configured settings using Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local):

Setting Type Purpose
enabled Boolean Whether tab rotation is active
delay Number (15–300) Seconds of inactivity before rotating
fullscreen Boolean Whether to enter fullscreen mode

This data is stored entirely on your local device and is never transmitted anywhere. You can clear it at any time by removing the extension.

Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions solely for its core functionality:

Permission Why It's Needed
tabs To list open tabs and switch the active tab during rotation
storage To save your settings locally between browser sessions
idle To detect system-level idle state and only rotate when you're away
activeTab To interact with the currently active tab when the extension is invoked

Network Requests

Kiosk Tab Rotator makes zero network requests. It does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or services. All functionality runs entirely within your browser.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected from any user, this concern does not apply.
